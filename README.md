# ⚖️ LexByte AI Lawyer

### AI-Powered Legal Research & Constitutional Intelligence Platform

LexByte AI Lawyer is an advanced legal assistant designed to help users understand Indian laws, constitutional provisions, legal rights, and legal procedures through an intuitive AI-powered interface.

Built for students, legal researchers, professionals, and citizens, LexByte combines constitutional knowledge with artificial intelligence to provide fast, accessible, and structured legal guidance.

🌐 **Live Demo:** https://lexbyte-ai-lawyer.web.app/

---

## 🚀 Features

### 🤖 AI Legal Assistant
- Ask legal questions in natural language
- Receive intelligent, context-aware responses
- Get simplified explanations of complex legal concepts

### 📖 Indian Constitution Knowledge Base
- Coverage of 179+ constitutional articles
- Organized by constitutional parts and categories
- Quick access to fundamental rights, duties, directives, and governance provisions

### 🔍 Smart Legal Search
- Search by article number
- Search by keywords or legal topics
- Instantly retrieve relevant constitutional provisions

### 📚 Legal Research Support
- Constitutional interpretation assistance
- Legal concept explanations
- Rights and obligations guidance
- Educational legal references

### 🎨 Modern User Experience
- Premium glassmorphism interface
- Responsive design for desktop and mobile
- Clean and professional legal-tech appearance

### ☁️ Web-Based Deployment
- Hosted on Firebase
- No installation required
- Accessible from any modern browser
